# About This Roadmap

This roadmap is public — and intentionally so.

LBM is being built in the open. We believe the best tools are shaped alongside the people who use them, not in secret. By keeping this roadmap visible, we're inviting you in: to understand where we're heading, to push back when something doesn't make sense, and to build with us if the direction excites you.

## Our Philosophy

LBM started as a tool for managing local business work without the overhead of Notion, Asana, or Linear. It's still that — but there's a longer arc.

We want LBM to be the most thoughtful local-first task manager ever built. Not the most feature-rich. The most **considered**. Every interaction, every keystroke, every data model decision should feel like someone thought carefully about it.

Building in public keeps us honest. When the roadmap is visible, we can't quietly drop features or silently change direction. We have to own our choices.

---

## A Note on Future Privacy

Some of this roadmap will become private over time.

As LBM grows toward a Pro/Cloud version, certain plans — pricing strategy, monetisation architecture, competitive positioning — will move to a private repository. This is standard for sustainable open-source products.

But the core product vision, feature plans, and design philosophy will stay public. That's the deal.

**In the early days, we're keeping everything open.** If you want to build with us, study the approach, or use LBM as a template for your own project — you're welcome. This is where we start.
